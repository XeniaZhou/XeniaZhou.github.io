"Experience With Nature"
1."I worked as the Panda keeper in Wolong,
Bifengxia and Hetaoping Panda Breeding Bases in Oct, 2016.
I cleaned Panda Houses, fed pandas and made special Panda
food. I also studied the knowledge of diseases, breeding
data and wild training conditions of pandas. That is an
unforgetable experience."
2."I also join in the group to investigate the living
condition of fish and birds in Jan, 2016 in Cambodia. Through
my skin was burnt under the strong sunshine, I encountered
different kinds of egrets, pelicans and catfishes, recording
their data and photoing them along with the peaceful lake and
gorgeous sunset, which was so meaningful and cheerful for my
heart of exploring nature."
"When First Approching..."
"When I started shooting my first microfilm,
I knew I needed to make it the best. I spent several days to
convince my classmate who I thought to be the best choice for
the main actor of joining my group -- it was a 'group' with
the only member: me. It was also the first time I became a
leader, recruiting actors and photographers, arrange the
shooting schedule and organize classmates to be the group
actors. Those trivial things I needed to deal with by myself
stimulated me to check the scenes again and again and refilmed
those inperfect fragments. Finally, mine small idea was refined
and finished by the whole class family. This first experience
is sweet."
